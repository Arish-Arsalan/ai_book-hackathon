Embodied AI Systems
Overview

Embodied AI systems combine physical form, sensors, actuators, and AI to operate in the real world.

Characteristics

Situatedness and embodiment

Perception-action loops

Learning and adaptation

Applications

Autonomous vehicles

Service robots

Industrial automation

Summary

Embodied AI systems leverage physical and computational integration for intelligent behavior.
