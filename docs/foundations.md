Foundations of Physical AI
Core Principles

Embodiment: Body influences intelligence

Perception-Action Loop: Continuous feedback between sensors and actuators

Situatedness: Context-aware decision making

Learning & Adaptation: Supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement, and imitation learning

Theoretical Foundations

Control Theory: PID, MPC

Artificial Intelligence: Symbolic reasoning, machine learning

Cognitive Science: Embodied cognition, learning from interaction

Summary

Mastering foundational principles is essential to understand Physical AI systems.
