Hardware for Physical AI
Mechanical Platforms

Mobile Robots: Wheeled, legged, aerial

Manipulators: Robotic arms, grippers

Humanoids: Balance, locomotion, manipulation

Sensors

Proprioceptive: Encoders, IMUs, force sensors

Exteroceptive: Cameras, LIDAR, ultrasonic

Tactile/Haptic: Touch, pressure sensors

Actuators

Electric Motors: DC, servo, stepper

Hydraulic: High power

Pneumatic: Fast, lightweight

Power & Communication

Batteries: Li-ion, Li-poly

Communication: ROS, CAN, Ethernet

Summary

Hardware design defines the capabilities and constraints of Physical AI systems.
