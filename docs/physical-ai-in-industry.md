Physical AI in Industry
Overview

Physical AI is transforming industries via automation, efficiency, and precision.

Key Applications

Manufacturing: robotic assembly, CNC automation

Logistics: warehouse robots, autonomous delivery

Healthcare: surgical robots, rehabilitation

Benefits

Increased efficiency

Reduced human risk

Scalability and consistency

Summary

Physical AI is a cornerstone of Industry 4.0, improving productivity and safety.
